About Us

We Are Making To Making You Different From Others

The CHS Tennis program has had the privilege of developing and working with a number of outstanding tennis athletes who had reached the professional level, such as Debbie Spence Nasim, who reached an all time high ranking of No.35 on WTA rankings, as well as many tennis athletes who have gone on to compete in Division I, II, & III colleges after graduating from CHS. With multiple league championships and a handful of CIF Championship victories, the most recent of which was the 2022 CIF Boys Tennis Champions, our tennis programs have consistently ranked among some of the strongest in the CIF Southern Section & the California State every season.

Faq's

General frequently asked questions

New students who wish to join the tennis team must participate in tennis tryouts. Typically, boy's and girl's tennis tryouts take place around mid-August, just before the start of the school year.

Girl's season take place in the fall, with games typically beginning in mid-August and continuing through early November. Boy's season takes place in the spring, with games typically beginning in the late-January time period. On game days for either boys or girls seasons, we typically conclude our home contests at approximately 6 p.m. On away games, we normally return to Cerritos High School between 6:30 p.m. and 7 p.m., depending on traffic and distance. Except on days when we have scheduled games, practice begins at 2 p.m. and ends at 4 p.m. on a daily basis.

Every new school year, all students who wish to play tennis at CHS must fill out and submit an athletic clearance packet. Students will not be permitted to try out for tennis or participate on the tennis team until the athletic clearance packet has been completed. Please visit https://www.cerritoshs.us/athleticclearance/" for the 2022-2023 season clearance. You can also go through our website, https://cerritoshs.us/, under the athletics tab.
